MORNING PRAYER – 1

Our father, who art in heaven, hallowed be thy name; thy kingdom come, thy will be done on earth as it is in heaven. Given us this day our daily bread, and forgive us our trespasses as we forgive those who trespass against us; and lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil. AMEN

MORNING PRAYER – 2

Almighty God my loving Father I love you with my whole heart and above all things I offer to you all my thoughts and actions and ask you to bless me during this day Help me so that I may know, love & serve you and be eternally happy with you in heaven. AMEN

PRAYER AFTER THE RECESS

We give you thanks, Almighty God, for all the benefits, which we have received, from your goodness. AMEN

PRAYER AFTER CLASS

Almighty God, infinite wisdom and source of all knowledge, grant that we may remember what we have learnt today. We offer to you our work and endeavours, our failure and success. Forgive us our shortcomings and give us the courage to follow the path of truth always. AMEN

PRAYER BEFORE THE EXAMINATION

O God, you are a loving Father, Full of wisdom and knowledge, you have created my mind, with all its faculties, Intellect, reason, Memory and understanding. At present, my place in your divine plan, is a student. The time has now come, when through this examination, I have to show, how much knowledge I possess, and how well, I know my subjects. Be with me, and all the other students. Help us, I humbly ask you, that we may not get confused, nervous or sick. Illumine our minds, that we may understand, the questions properly, and be able to answer them correctly. Bless all our teachers, parents, friends, and those who help us. AMEN
