Welcome To ST.XAVIERS'S SCHOOL

  1. Every candidate for admission must be introduced in person by one who will be responsible to the Principal for the child.
  2. New admissions are taken for Nursery, LKG, UKG, Class I to VII according to the availability of seats and on the basis of the written test and an interview. The merit of the student is the sole criterion of selection.
  3. The ideal age for starting in Nursery is 3 +. Only the parents are interviewed for their understanding of the type of education offered and their ability to co-operate with it.
  4. Correct name & date of birth are to be given in the Admission Forms & this, once registered cannot be altered.
  5. False declaration about a child’s age is disqualification for admission and subsequent discovery by the school authorities of such a false declaration would be grounds for dismissal.
  6. Anyone pressurizing the school authority for the Admission through recommendation will lead to disqualification.
