Welcome To ST.XAVIERS'S SCHOOL

St. Xavier's School, Balarampur is a unit of Jamshedpur Disociation Education Society. Reg. No. 529 of 2006-2007-6th Dec. 2006. It is situated on the left side of NH 32 leading to Balarampur town. In the year 2008, the school began with the nursery and lkg and every year one class will be added. It will be affiliated to the council for the Indian school certificate examination.

St. Xavier's School, Balarampur is a catholic school, establish and administered by the Roman Catholic Diocese of Jamshedpur. The responsibility for the administration and management of the school rests on the principal. The Bishop of Jamshedpur will appoint a managing committee to help and guide the administration for the welfare and development of school.

St. Xavier's school is open to all irrespective of religious, linguistic, cultural, and social affliations. As a Catholic Minority School, it draw inspiration and guidance from the life and teaching of Jesus Christ. Due importance will be given to the faith formation of the Catholics,Moral science and Value education for all the students.
