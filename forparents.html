Welcome To ST.XAVIERS'S SCHOOL

Parents are also requested

  1. To be a living example of the values taught and expected of their children.
  2. To keep the school authorities informed of any special problem, family circumstances or other relevant facts that are necessary for a proper understanding and guidance of their children.
  3. Not to bring/send tiffin, books/other articles etc. to their wards during school hours.
  4. Not to engage private tutors without consulting the Principal.
  5. Personal memo page should be carefully filled in by the parents at the beginning of the academic session. Change of residence / phone nos. should be notified to the school office immediately.
  6. Giving presents to the staff or other demonstrations of gratitude or goodwill in their honour is not allowed. Discourage your child to bring gifts for his/her classmates.
  7. Any communication (request or complaint) made by the parents must be addressed to the Principal/Sister-in-charge and not to the class teachers.
  8. Parents are particularly expected to sign the Principal’s or teacher’s remarks failure to do this may put their children to great inconvenience.
  9. Kindly see that the child attends the school in proper prescribe uniforms.
  10. Be patient with your children and encourage them.
  11. Parents are requested not to ask for leave for their wards to attend weddings and other social and religious functions during the term.
  12. Guardians or escorts will not accompany the students right up to the class room.
  13. Students must bring with them all the articles needed for the day. Guardians should not insist in the office/gate to forward articles/tiffin to the students during class hours.
  14. Parents and guardians are requested to specially note the following :
  15. They are requested to co-operate with the school at all times. If they have any problem, they are requested to meet the Principal/Sister-in-Charge individually to sort the problem for the good of their children.
  16. However it is not expected of our school parents/guardians.
  17. Private tuitions are discouraged. No teacher of St. Xavier’s School, Purulia is allowed to take tuitions for the students of this school.
  18. Children should do their homework, projects, charts etc. by themselves. Parents or their tutors should not do it for them. It is important that the children develop a habit of studying and working on their own.
  19. Criticism of teachers and the school authorities in front of their children by the parents should be avoided. Should they have any legitimate complaint, meet the Principal/Sister-in-charge according to the office time.
